Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working together

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web development services?

My services include custom web development, responsive design, performance optimization, SEO basics, testing, deployment, and post-launch support. I also offer ongoing maintenance and updates as needed.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. Through video calls, project management tools, and regular communication, I ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location. Time zone differences are managed effectively.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, WordPress, React, Vue.js, Node.js, and various databases. I also work with popular CMS platforms and e-commerce solutions.

How do you handle project revisions?

I include 2-3 rounds of revisions in most project packages. Additional revisions are handled at an hourly rate. I believe in getting it right, so I work closely with clients throughout the process to minimize major changes.
